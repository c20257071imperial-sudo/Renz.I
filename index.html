001  /  About

Who Is Renz?

Lorenz Imperial. Renz, if you've earned that. I don't hand out introductions like they're free — if you need a paragraph to figure me out, you're already overthinking it.

I move at my own pace. I show up when I want to, disappear when I need to, and I don't owe anyone an explanation for either. That's just how it is.

People mistake my silence for coldness. It's not. It's selection. I'd rather sit alone in quiet than be surrounded by noise that means nothing.

I build things, I drive at 2am, I wander into places I've never been, and I sit with my thoughts longer than most people are comfortable with. I don't need much — I just need the right things.

Who I Am.

01

I disappear when I feel overwhelmed.

No warning. No explanation. When things get too heavy, I shut down and pull back — not to hurt you, but because it's the only way I know how to handle it. I'll come back when I've processed it. Just don't make me feel guilty for needing that.

⚠   don't chase, just wait
02

Prefers being alone most of the time.

Don't take it personally — solitude isn't a punishment, it's just where I recharge. I function better alone than I do in a crowd. If you need constant company to feel secure, we're going to exhaust each other.

⚠   don't force the presence
03

Still give my all, even when it costs me.

I know when something is draining me. I see it coming. And I still show up fully anyway — because half-effort isn't something I'm capable of when I actually care. It's not a flex. It's a flaw I've made peace with.

⚠   don't mistake it for weakness
04

I stay quiet and observe everything.

I don't talk much in a room — but I notice everything. The way people act, what they don't say, how they carry themselves. Silence isn't emptiness for me. It's data. By the time I speak, I already know more than you think.

⚠   nothing gets past me
05

I choose peace over explaining myself.

If I have to convince you of my worth or defend every decision I make, I'd rather just go quiet. I don't argue to win — I disengage to survive. My peace isn't up for debate, and I won't lose it just to make someone else comfortable with who I am.

⚠   don't expect a debate
